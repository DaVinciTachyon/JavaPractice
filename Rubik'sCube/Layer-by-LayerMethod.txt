Layer-by-Layer Method

Step 1 - Good White Cross
  Algorithms
    Swap FUed and UFed
      F R' D' R F'2
    Swap FDed and UFed
      F' R' D' R F'2
    Swap FRed and UFed
      R' D' R F'2

Step 2 - Good White Corners
  Algorithms
    Swap RFDco and UFRco
      R' D' R
    Swap FRDco and UFRco
      F D F'
    Swap DFRco and UFRco
      R' D2 R D R' D' R
Take to the bottom if in wrong position

Step 3 - Second Layer
  Swap FUed and FRed
    U R U' R' U' F' U F
  Swap FUed and FLed
    U' L' U L U F U' F'
  Swap FRed and RFed
    U R U' R' U' F' U F - U2 - U R U' R' U' F' U F

Step 4 - Yellow Cross on Top
  Nothing to L to Horizontal Line to Cross (on Top)
    F R U R' U' F'
  L to Cross (on Top)
    F U R U' R' F'

Step 5 - Swap edges for good yellow cross
  Swap UFed and ULed
    R U R' U R U2 R' U

Step 6 - Position yellow corners
  Swap UBRco and UBLco and UFLco (anti-clockwise)
    U R U' L' U R' U' L

Step 7 - Orient Last Layer Correctly
  Rotate FRUco
    R' D' R D 2x or 4x until oriented properly
    U'
    Back to start until all corners are oriented
    U
    Permute all layers properly
